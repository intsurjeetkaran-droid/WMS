  WMS Pro - Warehouse Management System
  Non-Technical User Guide
============================================================

WHAT IS THIS?
─────────────
WMS Pro is a web-based Warehouse Management System that helps
you manage inventory, track orders, handle inbound shipments,
and monitor stock across multiple warehouses — all from a
browser.

------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO ACCESS THE APP
------------------------------------------------------------

Once the app is deployed, open your browser and go to the
URL provided by your administrator.

You will see a Login page. Use the credentials below.

WHAT EACH ROLE CAN DO
------------------------------------------------------------

  Super Admin       → Full access to everything. Can manage
                      users, settings, all modules.

  Warehouse Manager → Manages warehouses, approves inbound
                      shipments, oversees stock.

  Inventory Manager → Views and adjusts inventory levels,
                      manages products and suppliers.

  Staff             → Receives goods, processes inbound
                      shipments, updates stock.

  Dispatch Staff    → Picks, packs, and ships orders.

  Viewer            → Read-only access. Can view reports
                      and dashboards but cannot make changes.

------------------------------------------------------------
MAIN SECTIONS OF THE APP
------------------------------------------------------------

  Dashboard         → Overview of key stats: total stock,
                      pending orders, low stock alerts.

  Inventory         → See all products and their stock
                      levels across warehouses.

  Products          → Product catalog with SKUs, prices,
                      and supplier info.

  Orders            → Outbound customer orders. Track
                      status from pending to delivered.

  Inbound           → Incoming shipments (GRNs). Receive
                      and verify goods from suppliers.

  Suppliers         → Supplier directory and contact info.

  Warehouses        → Warehouse locations, zones, and
                      bin/rack structure.

  Stock Movement    → Full history of every stock change
                      (received, shipped, transferred, etc.)

  Reports           → Generate inventory and order reports.

  Alerts            → Low stock and expiry notifications.

  Audit Logs        → Who did what and when (admin only).

------------------------------------------------------------
COMMON TASKS
------------------------------------------------------------

  To check stock levels:
    → Go to Inventory → search by product name or SKU.

  To process a new order:
    → Go to Orders → click "New Order" → fill in customer
      details and select products.

  To receive a shipment:
    → Go to Inbound → find the GRN → click "Receive" →
      enter quantities received.

  To view low stock alerts:
    → Go to Alerts or check the Dashboard notifications.
